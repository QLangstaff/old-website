Hi, I'm Qali!

For those wondering, my name is pronounced like Cali. In Inuktitut, the traditional language of indigenous people inhabiting the Arctic (also known as Inuit), qali is one of many words to describe snow and specifically refers to the snow that builds up on tree branches. I was born on a very snowy night so my parents thought it was a perfect name for me.

I'm an engineering student at Western University and the Director of Recruitment at Western AI. I'm very interested in learning about tech and familiar with programming technologies including C++, Python, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

I created this website to showcase my skills through personal projects. It is still a work in progress so stay tuned for more content coming soon.
